FAQ
  

Common questions.

  
  
    
      
        Do I need an Alpaca account?         +       
      
For Simulation mode, no — the engine runs entirely on synthetic price data, no account needed. For Paper or Live trading, you'll need a free Alpaca brokerage account. Alpaca paper accounts are free and take about 5 minutes to set up at alpaca.markets.
    
    
      
        Does this guarantee profits?         +       
      
No. No trading system guarantees profits. Algorithmic trading involves significant risk and past simulated performance does not indicate future results. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose, and always start in simulation or paper mode before going live.
    
    
      
        How does the risk management work?         +       
      
Every trade is a bracket order — stop loss and take profit are submitted at the same time as the entry. Low risk sizes 1.5% of equity per trade with a 1.5% stop. Medium is 4% size / 3% stop. High is 8% size / 5% stop. The system also enforces a maximum number of simultaneous open positions per risk level.
    
    
      
        Where does my API key go? Is it safe?         +       
      
Your Alpaca API key is entered directly in the app and sent only to Alpaca's servers. It is never stored on our servers. You can use a paper-trading-only key for additional safety — Alpaca lets you create keys with restricted permissions.
    
    
      
        Can I cancel anytime?         +       
      
Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
